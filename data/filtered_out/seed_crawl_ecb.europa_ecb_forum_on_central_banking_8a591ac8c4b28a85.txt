ECB Forum on Central Banking The ECB Forum on Central Banking – the Sintra Forum – is an annual event organised by the European Central Bank and is held in Sintra, Portugal. It brings together central bank governors, academics, financial market representatives, journalists and others to exchange views on current policy issues and discuss the Forum’s key topic from a longer-term perspective. ECB Forum on Central Banking - 2026 The theme of the 2026 ECB Forum on Central Banking will be “Shaping Europe’s future: innovation, growth and stability”. The Forum takes place from 29 June to 1 July 2026 in Sintra. 2026 programme Young Economist Prize 2026 Every year the ECB offers PhD students in economics or finance the opportunity to share their research at the ECB Forum on Central Banking and win €10,000.
