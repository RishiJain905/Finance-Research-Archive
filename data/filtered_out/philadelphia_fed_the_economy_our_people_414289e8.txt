Experts in Key Areas
The Economy
We produce and compile research and data on the overall economy, banking, and financial markets.
Consumer Finance
We research how people earn, spend, save, and invest and how credit markets and payment systems affect the economy.
Community Development
We work to expand access to credit, jobs, education, and housing to foster economically resilient communities.
Macroeconomics
We research and compile data on growth, unemployment, inflation, and income for the overall U.S. economy.
Consumer Credit
We study consumer credit behavior and how consumer credit markets function and impact the economy as a whole.
Inclusive Growth
We support a strong and inclusive economy that provides economic opportunity for all people.
