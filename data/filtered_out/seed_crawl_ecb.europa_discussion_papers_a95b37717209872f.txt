Discussion papers Discussion papers are research-based publications that cover topics relevant to policy from a broad and balanced perspective. While based partly on original research, discussion papers place the analysis in the wider context of the existing literature and also explicitly address the topic from the policy point of view. Papers 1 to 20 inclusive were originally published as part of the ECB’s Working Paper Series. Availability: ECB Discussion Papers are available online only.
