Overview
The Bank’s mission is to maintain
monetary
and
financial
stability. Our market operations are some of our most important tools in delivering this mission.
This guide explains how and why we operate in markets, and how our operations work in practice.
Our Objectives
explains how we use the assets and liabilities on our balance sheet to deliver the Bank’s mission.
Our Tools
outlines the facilities that are available to firms, and how they can be accessed. We hope that this guide aids your understanding of our market operations and supports you in their use.
