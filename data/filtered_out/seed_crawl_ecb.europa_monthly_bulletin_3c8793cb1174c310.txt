Monthly Bulletin The Monthly Bulletin was published from January 1999 to December 2014, when it was replaced by the Economic Bulletin. Economic Bulletin Decision taken by the Governing Council on 19 December 2014 The Monthly Bulletin was published one week after each monetary policy meeting of the Governing Council. It explained the monetary policy decision and provided a detailed analysis of the current economic situation and risks to price stability.
