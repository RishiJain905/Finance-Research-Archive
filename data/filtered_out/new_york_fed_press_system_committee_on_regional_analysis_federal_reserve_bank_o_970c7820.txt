System Committee on Regional Analysis - FEDERAL RESERVE BANK of NEW YORK
Conference Announcement and Agenda
System Committee on Regional Analysis
November 1-3, 2017
Overview
This year’s annual meeting of the Federal Reserve System Committee on Regional Analysis is divided into three segments: a workshop on economic and financial surveys; a meeting covering topics related to community development, data collection, and economic measurement; and a conference with academic papers on a range of topics related to regional and spatial economics.
