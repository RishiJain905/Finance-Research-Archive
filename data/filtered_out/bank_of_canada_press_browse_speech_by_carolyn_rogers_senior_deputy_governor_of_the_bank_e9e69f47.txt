OTTAWA – On Thursday, March 26, 2026, Carolyn Rogers, Senior Deputy Governor of the Bank of Canada, will speak before the Brandon Chamber of Commerce.
Topic
Economic developments, monetary policy and affordability
Time
13:25 (Eastern Time)
Place
Victoria Inn
3550 Victoria Ave
Brandon, MB
Lock-Up
At 11:40 (ET), journalists are invited to review copies of the speech, under embargo, at the Bank’s head office in Ottawa. Please use the Bank of Canada Museum entrance, located at 30 Bank Street (corner of Bank and Wellington), and bring photo ID.
For security reasons, journalists wishing to attend must confirm their presence by contacting
Media Relations
before 14:00 (ET) on Wednesday, March 25, 2026. Those who have not registered will not be admitted to the lock-up.
The embargo will be lifted at 13:10 (ET).
Distribution
The Senior Deputy Governor’s text will be available on the Bank’s
website
at 13:10 (ET).
Media Availability
There will be no media availability for this event.
Audience Q&A
There will be a moderated Q&A period.
Webcast
Audio and video
webcasts
of the speech will be available.
Note
For more information, please contact
Media Relations
.
