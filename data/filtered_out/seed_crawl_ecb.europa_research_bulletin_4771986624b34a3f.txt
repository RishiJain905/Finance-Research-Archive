Research Bulletin The Research Bulletin features a selection of recent work on policy-relevant topics by ECB economists. Published on a monthly basis, the articles in the Research Bulletin are intended for a general audience. The views expressed in each article are those of the authors and do not necessarily represent the views of the European Central Bank and the Eurosystem. For information about the Research Bulletin articles, contact ecb-researchbulletin@ecb.int . Availability: ECB Research Bulletin articles are available online only, get updated on latest releases by RSS news feed .
