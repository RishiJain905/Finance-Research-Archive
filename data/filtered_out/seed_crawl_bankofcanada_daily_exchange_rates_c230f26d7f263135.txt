All Bank of Canada exchange rates are indicative rates only, obtained from averages of aggregated price quotes from financial institutions. For details, please read our full Terms and Conditions . The daily average exchange rates are published once each business day by 16:30 ET. In the presence of extraordinary market conditions or technical issues, the publication of exchange rates may be delayed. For details, see this backgrounder . Exchange rates are expressed as 1 unit of the foreign currency converted into Canadian dollars.
