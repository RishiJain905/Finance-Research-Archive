Integrated Trading Technology
NYSE Pillar is our integrated trading technology platform which enables member firms to connect to all NYSE equities and options markets using a standard protocol. Pillar is designed to improve efficiency and reduce complexity for customers, while enhancing consistency, performance and resiliency.
