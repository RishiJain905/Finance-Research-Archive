Treasury Non-Marketable Securities State and Local Government Series (SLGS) Securities Overview SLGS securities are offered for sale to issuers of state and local government tax-exempt debt to assist with compliance of yield restriction or arbitrage rebate provisions of the Internal Revenue Code. Series EE Savings Bonds Savings bonds that earn the same rate of interest (a fixed rate) for up to 30 years. Series I Savings Bonds Savings bonds that earn interest based on combining a fixed rate and an inflation rate.
