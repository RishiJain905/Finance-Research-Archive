Annual Report The Annual Report describes the tasks and activities of the European System of Central Banks (ESCB) and reports on the Eurosystem’s monetary policy. It is usually published in the spring of the following year and is presented by one of the ECB’s Executive Board members to the European Parliament at a public hearing. 2024 In figures 2.4 % Headline inflation declined towards our 2% target Headline inflation declined to 2.4% in December 2024 from 2.9% in December 2023, moving closer to target. More on inflation -100 basis points The ECB cut interest rates four times The reduction in the key ECB rates brought the deposit facility rate to 3.00% and its cumulative decrease over 2024 to 100 basis points.
