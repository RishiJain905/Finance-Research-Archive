Annual Report 2024 DYNAMIC | ENGAGED | TRUSTED Governor's foreword The last few years have been tough for Canadians and challenging for the Bank of Canada. But as 2024 ends, our economy is in much better shape. We didn’t get everything right. And households and businesses suffered under the burden of elevated inflation and higher interest rates. But it’s worth heralding 2024 as the year we returned inflation to target. 2024 by the numbers Mandate and planning framework Monetary policy Financial system Retail payments supervision Funds management Currency Managing the Bank Communications and outreach Global engagement Governance Financial results Financial statements In focus Driving decisions with data The Bank of Canada continues to evolve its operations in response to rapid changes in the data landscape. The Bank is making more effective use of available data from a broad, diverse and growing set of sources; reducing potential risks related to data and technology; and preparing for future priorities that use data, analytics and models. Making connections Members of the Bank of Canada’s Governing Council travelled to communities across the country in 2024, meeting with a wide range of people. These efforts are part of the Bank’s commitment to listen to Canadians and explain its work.
