Press Release Monthly Empire State Manufacturing Survey Release Schedule July 14, 2003 Note To Editors Due to the attention given to the monthly Empire State Manufacturing Survey conducted by the Federal Reserve Bank of New York, following is the anticipated release schedule. Please note that in some instances these dates differ from those announced previously. The public report will be available on the Bank's website at 8:30 a.m. on the 15th of each month. If that date is a weekend or holiday, the report will be posted on the first business day following. Registered press representatives who have access to the Bank's embargoed website will have access to the report at 8:00 a.m. on the date of release.
