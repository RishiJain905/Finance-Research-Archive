Research Visitor Programme ​The Bank of England runs a Visitor Programme with a view to actively build and promote collaboration between Bank of England staff and top-calibre researchers. In this section Bank of England Agenda for Research, 2025-2028 The digital pound Fintech The Future of Finance - our response Decision Maker Panel teams Research funding and fellowships Researchers We specifically focus on visitors with a strong track-record in research relevant to our policy tasks as a Central Bank and supervisor. During their one week visit a Research Visitor will give a seminar and interact with researchers and policy makers across the Bank.
