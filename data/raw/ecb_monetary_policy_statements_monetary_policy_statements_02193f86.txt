Monetary policy statements
The Governing Council takes its monetary policy decision every six weeks. Immediately after the meeting, the President and the Vice President of the ECB explain the decision at the press conference and answer questions from journalists. The press conference can be watched live at 14:45 CET and on demand shortly after.
